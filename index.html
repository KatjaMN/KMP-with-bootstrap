Playing with light

Opening the door and walking outside. Being temporarely unable to see because of the sun light flooding my eyes. Then starting to see details and more details. Something in between not seeing and seeing perfectly fascinates me. It is more of a feeling, than an everyday moment.

This is what I'm going after with my photography: the intuitive moment, where what is before the eyes is not as important as the feeling, that the light and the details wake in me.


To start a slideshow, you can push Play and pause it when you like.
